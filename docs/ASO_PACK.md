## 1. Positioning
Product: **QuizLoop**
Category: Games / Trivia
Audience: Students and casual users seeking quick daily brain challenges.
Core promise: "Play a fast daily quiz, keep your streak, and level up your brain."

## 9. Ratings and Reviews Strategy
- Trigger rating prompt only after positive moments (round win, streak milestone)
- Never request rating after ad interaction or loss
- In-app support link for unhappy users before review intent

## 11. Submission Checklist (ASO)
- App name/title finalized
- Subtitle/short description finalized
- Keywords field validated
- 8 screenshots exported per device class
- Feature graphic ready (Google Play)
- Privacy policy URL active
- Support URL active
- Category and content rating set

## 12. 30-Day ASO KPI Targets
- Store Listing Conversion Rate: >= 20%
- D1 Retention from store users: >= 30%
- D7 Retention: >= 12%
- Rating average: >= 4.3
- Review velocity: >= 20 reviews per 1,000 installs

## 13. Potential Payback (Linked to ASO Quality)
Assuming ASO improves conversion and lowers acquisition cost:
- Poor ASO CVR (10-12%): slower growth, payback often 6+ months
- Good ASO CVR (18-22%): moderate growth, payback often 3-4 months
- Strong ASO CVR (25%+): faster growth, payback can reach 1-3 months

These are scenario estimates, not guaranteed outcomes.
